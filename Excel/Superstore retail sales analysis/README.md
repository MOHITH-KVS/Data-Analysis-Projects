# 📊 Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

The Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence dashboard developed using Microsoft Excel. The objective of this project is to analyze sales, profit, customer behavior, discounts, product performance, and regional performance to identify the key factors affecting the company's profitability.

The dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor important KPIs, explore data interactively using slicers and timeline filters, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Although the company has achieved strong overall sales, overall profit is not increasing proportionally.

This project aims to answer business questions such as:

- Why is profit lower even when sales are increasing?
- Which products are generating losses?
- Which category is the most profitable?
- Which region needs improvement?
- How are discounts affecting profitability?
- Which customers and products contribute the most to revenue?

# 🔍 Business Insights

Based on the dashboard analysis:

- Technology is the highest profit-generating category.
- Furniture receives the highest average discount but generates the lowest profit.
- Tables is the largest loss-making sub-category.
- South region records the lowest sales performance.
- Consumer segment contributes the highest overall profit.
- Standard Class shipping generates the highest profit.
- November records the highest sales.
- December records the highest profit.
- Medium and High discounts are associated with negative profitability.
- Customer **AB-10105** generates the highest revenue.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are suggested:

- Reduce excessive discounts in the Furniture category.
- Review pricing strategy for the Tables sub-category.
- Increase marketing efforts in the South region.
- Focus on promoting high-margin Technology products.
- Retain high-value customers through loyalty programs.
- Optimize discount policies to improve overall profitability.
